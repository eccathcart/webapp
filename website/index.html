Introducing...

Eventure!

Eventure is a fast, simple app that's dedicated to finding events in New Zealand that you may enjoy attending.

When you open the app you'll be able to search by three different ways, which are: searching by the most popular, searching for nearby events or creating a custom search.

The best part though is that the app is absolutely...
FREE!
