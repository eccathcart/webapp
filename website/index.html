Introducing...

Eventure!

Eventure is a fast, simple app that's dedicated to finding events in New Zealand that you may enjoy attending.

When you open the app you'll be able to search by three different ways, which are: searching by the most popular, searching for nearby events or creating a custom search.

Once you've found an event that you like, you'll be able to see where it's happening, when it's happening and how much the tickets will cost.

The best part though is that the app is absolutely free and you can download it right now! Just scan the QR code in the middle and you'll be all set to go.
